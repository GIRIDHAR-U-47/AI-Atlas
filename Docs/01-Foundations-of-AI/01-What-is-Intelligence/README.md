# What is Intelligence?

This section explores the fundamental concept of intelligence and how it applies to artificial intelligence.

## Topics Covered

1. **Definition of Intelligence** - Understanding what intelligence means
2. **Rationality** - The concept of rational decision-making
3. **Intelligence vs Automation** - Distinguishing intelligent systems from automated ones
4. **Human vs Artificial Intelligence** - Comparing biological and machine intelligence
5. **Strong AI vs Weak AI** - Different levels of AI capability

## Learning Objectives

- Define intelligence in the context of AI
- Understand the principles of rational agents
- Differentiate between automation and true intelligence
- Compare human and artificial intelligence
- Distinguish between strong (general) and weak (narrow) AI
